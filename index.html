ABOUT US

Established in 2015 with a deep passion and love for baking, Eat Baked.inc began its journey to share this enthusiasm with the local community in the heart of Singapore.

Over the years, we have grown into a leading bakery and cake shop in Singapore, known for our commitment to quality, creativity, and excellence.
